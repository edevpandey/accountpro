Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024 August 20

Introduction

This privacy policy ("Policy") applies to your use of this app (Account Sir). This app does not represent any government entity. This app is built solely for personal usages. This Policy describes the information we collect through the App and how we use it.

This app requires access to your device's external storage to backup and restore database. You can grant or revoke this permission in your device settings.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • User Account Information: When you sign in with your Google account, we collect your basic account information such as your name, profile photo and email address. This information is used solely for account management purposes within the App and is not shared with any third party.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, such as the device model, operating system version, and a unique device identifier. This information is used for technical purposes, such as analyzing app performance and troubleshooting issues.

Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect information about your device and app usage to serve you relevant ads. We do not have control over the data collected or used by Google AdMob. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and how to opt out of personalized advertising by visiting https://www.google.com/ads/preferences/html/blocked-cookies.html.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App;
  • To manage your account;
  • To analyze how you use the App;
  • To personalize your experience within the App;
  • To communicate with you about the App, including updates, security alerts, and support; and
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third party except for Google AdMob as described above. We may share anonymized and aggregated information about user activity with third-party analytics providers to help us improve the App.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Your Choices

You can access or update your account information at any time by signing in to your Google account. If you want to delete your information, please contact us. We will take steps to delete the information from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. We will take steps to delete the information from our servers.

Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact us.
